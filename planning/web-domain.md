# Web Domain Selection

## Decision

**gl.je** - General Ledger Journal Entry

- Cost: $51.80/year (consistent, no premium renewal trap)
- Length: 5 characters
- 5-year total: $259

## Why gl.je

Three layers of meaning:

1. **GL** = General Ledger
2. **JE** = Journal Entry (fundamental unit of double-entry bookkeeping)
3. **.je** = Jersey (British Crown dependency known as a major financial services center)
